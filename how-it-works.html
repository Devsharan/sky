How it Works

Procedure to book slots through BookYourGround.com :

  • Log on to www.BookYourGround.com
  • Select the desired City, Sport, Date
  • Choose 'Venue' and 'Time slot' as per your preference
  • Click on 'Continue'
  • Crosscheck booking details and the amount payable in the Order Summary
  • Enter your Email Id and Mobile no. in order to get the booking confirmation email and SMS
  • Read and Check 'I Agree to the Terms and Conditions'
  • Click on 'Proceed to Payment' to go ahead with your booking
  • Choose the preferred Mode of Payment
  • Enter your Card number and other card details and click on 'Pay'
  • You will receive a booking confirmation number by email and SMS
  • In a rare case, if you do not receive the email or SMS, call our support number to get a new confirmation
  • You can contact us on (+91) 80958 87000, write to us at support@bookyourground.com for assistance
